Eyewear Size Guide

Understanding frame measurements can help you find the perfect fit. Most frames have three numbers printed on the inside temple arm: [Lens Width] - [Bridge Width] - [Temple Length].

Measurement Description Typical Range
Lens Width The horizontal width of each lens. 40-60 mm
Bridge Width The distance between the lenses. 14-24 mm
Temple Length The length of the arm from hinge to tip. 120-150 mm
General Size Guidelines:
  • Small (S): Lens Width 40-48mm, Total Frame Width < 130mm
  • Medium (M): Lens Width 49-54mm, Total Frame Width 130-138mm
  • Large (L): Lens Width 55-58mm, Total Frame Width 139-145mm
  • Extra Large (XL): Lens Width > 58mm, Total Frame Width > 145mm

Note: These are general guidelines. Fit can also depend on frame shape and personal preference.
